FAQ

> Who can participate?
Students from any undergraduate or graduate program interested in AI and Data Science.
> Registration fee?
The hackathon is completely free for all selected teams.
> Will food and refreshments be provided?
Yes. food and refreshments will be provided to all the participants.
> What is the reporting time?
Details will be shared with the selected participants via email.
> Will there be any certificates?
Yes, all participants will receive participation certificates, and winners will receive merit certificates.
> Is Guardian/Parents allowed?
No, guardians/Parents are not allowed inside the hackathon premises.
> Will OD recommendation letters be provided?
No, On-Duty (OD) recommendation letters will not be provided.
> Do I need to bring my own laptop?
Yes, all participants must bring their own laptops and necessary peripherals.
> Is On-Spot Registration Available?
No, only pre-registered and selected teams can participate.
> Will transportation be provided?
Yes, transportation will be provided exclusively from Tambaram.
